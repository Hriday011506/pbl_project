AI-Driven Predictive Inventory & Dynamic Pricing System

An AI-powered retail optimization system that predicts hyperlocal demand shifts using external signals like weather, events, and social trends, and dynamically adjusts inventory and pricing in real-time to maximize revenue and reduce stock inefficiencies.

Problem Statement

Traditional retail systems fail to predict sudden local demand shifts and adjust prices dynamically, leading to stockouts, overstock, lost revenue, and reduced profit margins. Current ERP/POS systems rely only on historical sales and fixed reorder rules without considering external demand signals.

Proposed Solution

The AIDP System integrates historical sales data with external signals such as weather forecasts, regional events, news trends, and social media signals. Using ML models like LSTM, Transformer, and Gradient Boosting, the system predicts SKU-level demand and applies a dynamic pricing engine to optimize profitability while minimizing waste.

Results & Impact

Forecast Accuracy 92%

Improved demand forecasting accuracy, reduction in stockouts, optimized pricing strategies, and better financial scenario simulations for retail decision-making.
